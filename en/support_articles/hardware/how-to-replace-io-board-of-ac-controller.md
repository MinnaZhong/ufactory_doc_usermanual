# How to replace IO board of AC controller

1. Follow the video to disassemble the controller.  
[How to disassemble the controller?](https://drive.google.com/drive/folders/1LiDyIoOXd-MtC4zW8miXUTSSDuxyNhir?usp=sharing)

2. Remove 19 screws, and unplug 8 cables.
**Please mark the wire sequence properly. If the cable is plugged incorrectly, the IO board may be burned out.**
![](../assets/io_board_1.png)

3. Remove 6 cables.
![](../assets/io_board_2.png)

4. Remove 2 cables.
![](../assets/io_board_3.png)

5. Replace a new IO board.
